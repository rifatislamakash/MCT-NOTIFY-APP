The Story Behind MCT Notify

Programming was never just a subject for me; it was a calling. From the moment I wrote my first line of code, something clicked inside me. I knew I had found my true path.

Life, however, took an interesting turn when I joined the Multimedia and Creative Technology (MCT) department. Initially, it felt like a detour from software engineering. But as time passed, I realized that technology and creative design are deeply connected. My passion for building systems and writing code never faded; it simply found a new, creative space to grow.

I noticed that my fellow MCT classmates were constantly missing class updates, rushing to find routines, or struggling to contact faculty members. Critical updates were scattered across different groups, which felt like a problem that shouldn't exist today. We needed a central home for our academic schedules, routines, and announcements.

That's when I decided to build a solution myself. MCT Notify was created to solve this communication gap. It is a tool made specifically for us, built with care to keep our student life organized and stress-free.

This app is proof that passion, when channelled with purpose, can create something that truly matters. To everyone who uses this: this platform is for you. I hope it helps you stay ahead and focus on what truly matters in your creative journey.

"Build things that matter. Code with heart. Impact people with purpose."

MD. RIFAT HOSSAIN | MCT Notify Creator

Our Purpose

MCT Notify was created to solve the real communication gap faced by MCT students at Daffodil International University. It centralises all academic information, from notices and schedules to routines and faculty details, into one clean, fast, and beautiful platform.

Key Features

Smart Notices

Urgent & general notices with push notifications, file attachments, and course targeting.

Schedule Hub

Class schedules for quizzes, presentations, lab works and more, always up to date.

Weekly Routine

Your complete class routine, day-by-day, with room numbers and assigned teachers.

Faculty Directory

Detailed faculty profiles with designation, room, assigned courses, and contact info.

Course Materials

Download lecture slides, PDFs, and study resources, all in one place.

Groups & Channels

Quick links to WhatsApp, Telegram, and Facebook groups for your courses.

Course Management

Enroll in your courses, view credits, and manage your academic profile.

Push Notifications

Real-time Firebase push notifications, even when the app is closed in the background.
